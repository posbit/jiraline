Goals and mission

Our mission is to make work with Jira more fluent. We are developers and do that for developers. We don`t want to switch beetween browser and our natural environment which is terminal. We do almost everything from command line, and we feel good with that.

Goals are pretty straigth - implement all functionality provides by Jira API.

Features

What can you do from command line:
  • comment
  • assign
  • list transitions
  • do transistions
